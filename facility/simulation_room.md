# Simulation Room

## Opening Hours

**Regular opening hours**

  Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays

```
9:00 AM-12:00 PM and 1:00 PM-5:00 PM

if you need technical assistance from UNF staff
 OR
if you do not have an access card to enter the UNF
```

**Outside regular hours**

```
7 days a week, 24 hours a day

if you do not need technical assistance from UNF staff
 AND
if you have an access card to enter the UNF
```


## Notes
- There is no fee for using the simulation room. However, it must be used in connection with ongoing projects at the UNF or in preparation for them.

- Unlike MRI reservations, no form is required to use the simulation room.

- When using the simulation room, it is important to ask the person how they are feeling, as some people are claustrophobic.

- Researchers must be present during the MRI simulation session to respond to the needs and requests of their participants.

- Researchers are responsible for operating and preparing the simulator, as well as the equipment needed for each session.

- Inform UNF staff if any problem is noticed with any equipment.
