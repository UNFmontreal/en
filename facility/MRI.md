# MRI

![MRI](/images/documentation/MRI_prisma.jpg)

The UNF is equipped with a Siemens 3T Prisma fit MRI. This MRI is located within the Institut universitaire de gériatrie de Montréal (IUGM). Movements within the UNF facilities are therefore subject to safety and distancing guidelines in order to best protect the residents and all employees.

## Opening hours

**Regular opening hours**

Monday to Friday, except holidays: 9:00 AM–12:00 PM and 1:00 PM–5:00 PM

**Outside regular hours**

Midday, weekends, evenings, nights, and holidays.

If you wish to access the MRI room outside of normal opening hours, please send an email to **[support.unf](mailto:support.unf@criugm.qc.ca?subject=Demande_Accès_MRI_Off-Hours)**.
