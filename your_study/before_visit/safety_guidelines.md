# Safety Training

Training on the safety measures to follow in an environment with a high magnetic field is **mandatory** for all UNF members (i.e., operators, technical staff, researchers, graduate students and postdoctoral fellows, and research assistants) and IUGM-associated personnel, as well as for anyone else who is, or may be, required to visit the UNF premises.

Visit the [__MRI Safety Training__](https://limesurvey.unf-montreal.ca/index.php/158719) page to register. The safety officer will inform you of the next training date.

- The course is offered approximately once a month.

- Future dates can be found on the website.

- The course is free and lasts approximately 1 hour.

This training includes:

- Basics of magnetic fields

- Safety guidelines to follow in an MRI room

- Safety guidelines to follow (COVID-19)

- Emergency procedures training

- Video excerpts on safety measures to follow in an MRI room

- Tour of the UNF

- Screening form for risk factors associated with exposure to a high magnetic field

- Question period
