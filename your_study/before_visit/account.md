# Accounts

In order to carry out your study at the UNF, you need to obtain a user account.
This account will allow you to make a reservation (MRI, simulation room or testing room) using the __[reservation calendar](https://reservation.unf-montreal.ca/Web/dashboard.php)__ and will give you access to the computing servers.

We also encourage you to ask to be added to the __[UNF slack](https://mic-unf.slack.com/)__. This is a chat where everyone can ask questions and help each other.

To obtain your account, send an email to the following address __[support.unf](mailto:support.unf@criugm.qc.ca?subject=Inscription-Serveurs_UNF)__.

Please also include the following information:

* Your full name, address and (daytime) phone number.

* Your email address (the one that will be used for reservation confirmations)

* The full name, address and phone number of the principal investigator
of the research project (if different from 1.)

The reservation system generates automatically sent emails. To prevent these from ending up in your spam folder, make sure to add the address reservationunf@criugm.qc.ca to your list of approved senders.
