# Emergency Codes

In case of an emergency at the hospital or research center, the security officer in charge announces the appropriate emergency code. The internal emergency team acts according to the protocol established by the hospital.

:::{important}
Within the UNF premises, exceptional precautions apply due to the high magnetic field — hospital staff are not always familiar with procedures specific to an imaging unit.
:::

:::{danger}
**Call 5555 for any emergency**

Identify: **what** the emergency is · **where** it is located · **who** you are
:::
