# Medical emergency

:::{danger}
**Call 5555 for any medical emergency**

Identify: **what** the emergency is · **where** it is · **who** you are
:::

Security will follow the protocol set by the hospital. Typically, the coordinating nurse and the head of mechanical operations will respond first.

:::{important}
Within the UNF premises, exceptional precautions apply due to the high magnetic field — hospital staff are not always familiar with procedures specific to an imaging unit.
:::

## Procedure

1. **Evacuate the MRI room and close the doors.**
   This prevents anyone unaware of the danger from entering the room.

2. **Evacuate the UNF area.**
   Participants, visitors, and staff members must leave the premises and move to an unaffected area.

3. **Assist the emergency personnel.**
   Depending on the type of emergency, remain available to help as needed.

4. **Direct responders to the affected area.**
   The UNF team knows the premises better than external responders — guide them as needed.

## Equipment

:::{note}
The medical equipment is located in the cabinet in the participant preparation area. The pharmacy can provide medication supplies if necessary.
:::
