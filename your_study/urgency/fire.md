# Fire

:::{note}
In the event of a fire in the UNF area, the internal intervention brigade follows the protocol appropriate to the situation, as determined by the hospital.

Once the situation has been identified and confirmed, emergency measures will be followed according to the guidelines determined by the hospital and the procedures manual.

Within the UNF premises, exceptional precautions may be taken due to the high magnetic field, as hospital staff are not familiar with emergency procedures in an imaging unit.
:::

1. **Evacuate the MRI room and close the doors.**

   This will prevent anyone unaware of the danger of a high magnetic field from entering the room.

2. **Evacuate the UNF area.**

   Participants, visitors, and staff must leave the premises to reach an area not affected by the emergency situation.

3. **Assist emergency personnel.**

   Depending on the type of emergency, be available to help as needed.

4. **Direct emergency responders to the required area.**

   The UNF team is more familiar with the UNF area than the emergency responder; be available to help as needed.
